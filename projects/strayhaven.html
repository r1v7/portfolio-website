Stray Haven

Project Overview

Stray Haven is a mobile application designed to connect stray animals with loving homes. The app provides a seamless experience for users to browse adoptable pets, track adoption status, receive notifications about rescued animals, and manage their adoption journey.

The compassionate green-themed design symbolizes growth and care, creating an emotional connection with users while maintaining a clean and intuitive interface. The UI/UX was carefully crafted to ensure accessibility for diverse user groups.

Design Process

The design process began with extensive user research, including interviews with pet adopters and shelter staff to understand pain points in the current adoption process. User personas were created to guide the design decisions, ensuring that the final product would meet the needs of various user types.

Wireframes were developed to establish the app's information architecture and core user flows, followed by high-fidelity mockups that incorporated the visual design elements. User testing was conducted at various stages to refine the interface and improve the overall user experience.

Key Features

  • Pet discovery with detailed profiles and filtering options
  • Real-time notifications about adoption status and rescued animals
  • Stray animal reporting with location tracking and photo uploads
  • Pet supplies marketplace supporting animal rescue operations
  • User profiles with adoption history and impact statistics
  • Adoption tracking with application status monitoring

Technologies Used

  • Figma for UI/UX design
  • ChatGPT for Logo
  • Canva for icons

Challenges & Solutions

  • Creating an emotional yet functional design language
  • Balancing feature richness with simplicity for diverse users
  • Integrating location-based services with user privacy
  • Designing engagement loops for continued participation

Outcomes & Learnings

  • Improved understanding of user needs and preferences
  • Developed expertise in compassionate-focused design
  • Created a scalable platform for future community features
